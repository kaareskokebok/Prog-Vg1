Energy in Natural Sciences

Introduction

Energy is a fundamental concept in natural sciences, encompassing various forms and principles that govern the physical world. It is the capacity to do work and is essential in all scientific disciplines.

Forms of Energy

Energy exists in several forms, including:

  • Kinetic Energy
  • Potential Energy
  • Thermal Energy
  • Chemical Energy
  • Nuclear Energy

Energy Conversion

Energy can be converted from one form to another. For example, chemical energy in food is converted to kinetic energy when we move.
